Step 2: Browse listings on Redfin or Zillow

Navigate to any property listing page on redfin.com or zillow.com.

Step 3: Click the bookmarklet

Click "📋 Grab Listing" in your bookmarks bar. It will:

1. Read the page for price, beds, baths, sqft, year built, heating, AC, garage, open house info
2. Copy structured JSON to your clipboard
3. Show a confirmation popup with what it found

Step 4: Import into House Scorer

Open house-scorer.html, click "Import JSON", but instead of selecting a file:

1. Create a .json file — open Notepad, paste the clipboard contents, save as listing.json
2. Or use the "Paste Listing" button (being added to House Scorer) to paste directly

What it captures: address, price, beds, baths, sqft, year built, heating type, central air, garage spaces, attached/detached, lot size, open house info, listing URL, town name.

What you still rate manually: kitchen quality, entertaining space, renovation quality, street/setting — the subjective stuff.

Supported sites

Redfin — full support (price, beds, baths, sqft, year, heating, AC, garage, lot, open house)
Zillow — full support (same fields)
Others — not yet, but can be added
